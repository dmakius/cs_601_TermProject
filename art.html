My Newest Hobby

I only started two years ago when the pandemic lockdowns started. I currently only paint with acrylics but would like to branch out to watercolors and oil paints. I usually sketch an image before I paint it. As of now only my paintings are here and I plan on adding my sketches in the near future.


I tend to paint a combination of following tutorials on youtube and whatever is in my head. I enjoy painting landscapes that capture the life and action in a moment. I dont enjoy painting portraits as I find them boring. Regardless of what I paint, the most important thing is the colors that I use. I always perfer bright colors over darker colors. The world is a hard place so if I am going to paint it I might as well make it collorfull.
